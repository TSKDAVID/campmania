## 3. Rental period

3.1. The rental period begins on the delivery date and ends on the return date specified above.

3.2. The Lessee shall make rented items available for return pickup at the agreed address on the return date.

3.3. Late returns shall be charged at the daily rental rate for each additional day until items are returned.

---

## 4. Delivery and payment

4.1. The Lessor shall deliver items to the Lessee's address in Tbilisi on the agreed delivery date.

4.2. **Payment method:** Cash on delivery. The Lessee shall pay the total amount due in cash at the time of delivery.

4.3. **Delivery fee:** 5 GEL, included in the order total.

4.4. **Return pickup:** Free. The Lessor shall collect items at the Lessee's address on or before the return date.

---

## 5. Identity verification

5.1. At delivery, the Lessee shall present a valid **Georgian national ID card** or **passport**.

5.2. The Lessor shall verify the Lessee's identity against this agreement. This procedure protects both parties and is standard for equipment rental.

5.3. The Lessor does not retain copies of identification documents beyond what is necessary for the rental record, in accordance with the Privacy Policy.

## 7. Lessee responsibilities

The Lessee agrees to:

7.1. Use rented items only for their intended purpose (outdoor recreation and hiking).

7.2. Not sublease, lend, or transfer items to third parties without written consent.

7.3. Not use items for commercial guiding or rental without written consent.

7.4. Return all items complete with all accessories (poles, stakes, stuff sacks, etc.).

7.5. Return items in reasonably clean condition (free of excessive dirt, mud, or odor).

7.6. Report any damage, loss, or malfunction immediately to the Lessor.

7.7. Not modify, repair, or alter rented items without written consent.

7.8. Use items in accordance with manufacturer instructions and safety guidelines.

---

## 8. Prohibited uses

Rented items shall not be used for:

- Mountaineering or climbing requiring certified safety equipment beyond the item's rating
- Activities exceeding the manufacturer's stated limits
- Any illegal purpose
- Conditions clearly beyond the item's design (e.g., 3-season tent in severe winter storms)

---

## 9. Condition and inspection

9.1. Items are delivered in the condition grade stated on the product listing (Excellent or Good).

9.2. The Lessee shall inspect items at delivery and notify the Lessor of any issues before signing.

9.3. At return pickup, the Lessor shall inspect items on-site. Normal wear from intended use is expected and acceptable.

---

## 10. Damage and loss

10.1. The Lessee is responsible for loss, theft, or damage beyond normal wear during the rental period.

10.2. If damage occurs, the Lessor shall assess repair or replacement cost and communicate with the Lessee before charging.

10.3. Deductions from any security deposit shall be itemized in writing within 48 hours of return inspection.

10.4. If no deposit was collected, the Lessee remains liable for damage costs as assessed by the Lessor.

---

## 11. Security deposit

11.1. Where applicable, a refundable security deposit is collected in cash at delivery.

11.2. The deposit is returned within **48 hours** of return pickup, minus any authorized deductions for damage or missing items.

11.3. The Lessee shall receive written notice of any deposit deductions with explanation.

---

## 12. Cancellation

12.1. Cancellations 48+ hours before scheduled delivery: no charge.

12.2. Cancellations within 48 hours of delivery: up to 50% of rental total may be charged.

12.3. No-show at delivery: full rental charge may apply.

See [Refund Policy](/pages/refund-policy) for details.

---

## 13. Limitation of liability

13.1. The Lessor is not liable for injury, accident, or loss arising from the Lessee's use of rented items.

13.2. The Lessee assumes all risk associated with outdoor activities.

13.3. The Lessor's liability is limited to the rental fee paid for the affected items.
