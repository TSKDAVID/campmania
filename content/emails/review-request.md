# Review Request Email

**Trigger:** 3 days after return pickup completed
**Tool:** Judge.me automated review request (primary) OR manual/Klaviyo

---

## Subject line (Judge.me default)

```
How was your rental with [Your Brand]?
```

---

## Email body

```
Hi [CUSTOMER_NAME],

We hope your recent adventure was amazing!

You rented:
[ITEM_LIST]

We'd love to hear about your experience. Your review helps other hikers in Tbilisi find quality gear.

[LEAVE A REVIEW BUTTON → Judge.me link]

It takes about 60 seconds. Photo reviews are especially helpful!

As a thank you, use code COMEBACK10 for 10% off your next rental.

Planning another trip? Browse our gear:
https://[yourbrand].com/collections/all

Happy trails,
The [Your Brand] Team
```

---

## Judge.me configuration

1. **Settings → Review requests → Timing:** 3 days after fulfillment/return tag
2. **Custom question:** "What type of trip did you use this gear for?" (day hike, overnight, multi-day trek, family camp)
3. **Enable photo reviews:** Yes
4. **Email template:** Paste body above into Judge.me custom template

---
