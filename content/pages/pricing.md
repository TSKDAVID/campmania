## How rental pricing works

Each item has a **daily rate** and a **weekly rate** (7 days). Weekly rates offer a discount compared to renting day-by-day.

| Rental period | How it is priced |
|---------------|------------------|
| 1–6 days | Daily rate × number of days |
| 7 days | Weekly rate (discounted) |
| 8+ days | Weekly rate + daily rate for extra days |

Prices are shown in **GEL (Georgian Lari)** and displayed on each product page when you select your dates.

## Payment

**Cash on delivery only** at launch.

- You pay the full rental amount in cash when we deliver your gear
- No online payment or card required at checkout
- Have the exact amount ready (shown in your order confirmation email)

---

## Security deposits

Some high-value items may require a refundable security deposit, collected in cash at delivery. If applicable, the deposit amount is shown on the product page and order confirmation.

Deposits are returned within **48 hours** of return pickup, after we inspect the gear.

## Rent vs. buy

| Item | Buy (approx.) | Rent 3 days | Rent 7 days |
|------|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| 2-person tent | 400–800 GEL | ~105 GEL | ~85 GEL |
| 65L backpack | 500–900 GEL | ~90 GEL | ~70 GEL |
| Sleeping bag + pad | 300–600 GEL | ~75 GEL | ~60 GEL |

Renting makes sense for occasional trips, trying gear before buying, or when traveling light to Georgia.
