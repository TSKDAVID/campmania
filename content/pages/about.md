## Our story

[Your Brand] started with a simple frustration: great hiking gear is expensive, and most of it sits in closets between trips.

We are hikers based in Tbilisi who believe everyone should have access to quality outdoor equipment — without the cost and commitment of buying everything new. Whether you are planning your first overnight hike or gearing up for a multi-day trek in the Caucasus, we are here to help.

## Our mission

Make outdoor adventures accessible, affordable, and effortless for everyone in Tbilisi and across Georgia.

We do this by:
- Offering **quality gear** from trusted brands, maintained to high standards
- Keeping prices **affordable** with daily and weekly rental rates
- Delivering to your **door** — and picking up when you are done
- Running a **transparent, professional** rental process you can trust

## Based in Tbilisi

We know the trails around Georgia — from Kazbegi and Svaneti to Borjomi and the hills above Tbilisi. Our gear is chosen for the conditions you will actually face: variable weather, alpine terrain, and multi-day routes.

We serve all Tbilisi districts with door delivery and free return pickup.

## Sustainability

Renting instead of buying reduces waste. One quality tent shared across dozens of adventures has a fraction of the environmental impact of everyone buying their own. When you rent with us, you are making a practical and sustainable choice.
