Renting hiking gear with [Your Brand] is simple, transparent, and designed around your schedule. Here is exactly what to expect from booking to return.

## The process in four steps

### Step 1: Choose your gear and dates

Browse our catalog of tents, backpacks, sleeping systems, and more. On each product page, select your rental start and end dates using the date picker. You will see the total price in GEL before adding to cart.

You can rent individual items or choose a bundle for better value.

### Step 2: Complete your booking

At checkout:
- Enter your Tbilisi delivery address
- Select your preferred delivery date and time window
- Review your order total (rental + 5 GEL delivery)
- Agree to the Rental Agreement checkbox
- Place your order — no online payment required

**Payment method:** Cash on delivery. You pay when we hand over your gear.

### Step 3: Receive your rental agreement by email

Immediately after booking, you will receive:
- Order confirmation with rental dates and items
- Link to your **Rental Agreement** — please read it before delivery day
- Delivery details: date, time window, and address
- Amount due in cash at delivery

**Before delivery day, please:**
- Read the rental agreement sent to your email
- Have your **valid government-issued ID** ready (Georgian ID card or passport)
- Prepare the cash amount shown in your confirmation

### Step 4: Delivery day

Our team arrives at your address within your selected time window.

**At delivery, we will:**
1. Present your gear for inspection — check everything together
2. Verify your ID (quick check, takes under a minute)
3. Have you sign the rental agreement (paper copy)
4. Collect payment in cash
5. Hand over your gear

If anything is not as expected, tell us before we leave. We want you fully satisfied before your trip.

### Step 5: Enjoy your adventure

Use the gear for your hike, camp, or outdoor trip. If you have questions during your rental, contact us via phone, WhatsApp, or chat.

### Step 6: Return pickup — free

Before your rental end date, we will contact you to confirm a pickup time. Our team comes to your address, inspects each item on-site, and collects the gear.

**At return, we check:**
- All items returned complete (poles, stakes, stuff sacks, accessories)
- General condition — normal wear is expected
- Any damage beyond normal use (covered in rental agreement)

Return pickup is always free. There is no extra charge.

---

## What about deposits?

If a security deposit applies to your rental, it will be listed on your order confirmation. Deposits are collected in cash at delivery and returned after we inspect returned gear, typically within 48 hours of pickup.
