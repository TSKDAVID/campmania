## Booking and dates

**What is the minimum rental period?**
Most items can be rented for as little as 1 day. Bundles may have a 2-day minimum. The date picker on each product page shows available periods.

**Can I extend my rental?**
Yes, if the item is available. Contact us before your rental end date and we will adjust your booking. Extensions are charged at the daily rate for additional days.

**How far in advance can I book?**
Up to 90 days in advance. We recommend booking at least 2 days ahead to secure your preferred delivery date.

**Can I cancel my booking?**
Yes. Cancellations made 48+ hours before delivery receive a full refund of any prepaid amount. Within 48 hours, a cancellation fee may apply. See our [Refund Policy](/pages/refund-policy).

## Delivery

**What areas of Tbilisi do you deliver to?**
All Tbilisi districts. Delivery is a flat 5 GEL regardless of location within the city.

**What are your delivery hours?**
Tuesday through Saturday, 10:00–18:00. You choose your preferred time window at checkout.

**How soon can I get delivery?**
Order by [CUTOFF_TIME] for next-day delivery (Tuesday–Saturday). Same-day delivery is not available at launch.

**What if I am not home for delivery?**
Contact us to reschedule. One free reschedule is included. Repeated missed deliveries may incur a 5 GEL re-delivery fee.

**Can I add delivery instructions?**
Yes. Use the order notes field at checkout for gate codes, building entrance details, and contact phone for the driver.

## Payment

**How do I pay?**
Cash on delivery. You pay the full rental amount in cash when we deliver your gear. No online payment is required at checkout.

**How much cash should I prepare?**
The exact amount is shown in your order confirmation email. Please have the precise amount ready.

**Do you accept card payments?**
Not at launch. Cash on delivery only. Card payments may be added in the future.

**Is there a security deposit?**
Some high-value items may require a refundable deposit, collected in cash at delivery. If applicable, the amount is shown on the product page. Deposits are returned within 48 hours of return pickup.

## ID and rental agreement

**Why do I need to show ID?**
A quick ID verification confirms your identity and protects both you and us. It is standard practice for equipment rental and takes under a minute.

**What ID is accepted?**
Georgian national ID card or valid passport.

**What is the rental agreement?**
A simple contract outlining your responsibilities, our responsibilities, and terms for damage, returns, and payment. We email it to you immediately after booking so you can read it before delivery.

**Do I sign at delivery?**
Yes. You sign a paper copy at delivery after reviewing the gear together. The email version sent after booking is for your advance review.

**Is the ID check safe?**
Absolutely. We only verify your identity — we do not copy or store your ID beyond what is needed for the rental record. Your information is handled per our [Privacy Policy](/pages/privacy-policy).

## Returns

**Is return pickup really free?**
Yes. We come to your address and collect the gear at no extra charge.

**How do I schedule return pickup?**
We contact you 1 day before your rental end date to confirm a pickup time. You can also reach us to schedule.

**What condition should gear be in at return?**
Reasonably clean — shake out dirt, empty pockets, wipe down cooking gear. Normal trail wear is expected and acceptable.

**What if I return gear late?**
Late returns are charged at the daily rate for each additional day. Contact us if you need an extension before your rental ends.

**What if something is damaged?**
Report damage when we pick up. Minor wear is expected. Damage beyond normal use is covered in the [Rental Agreement](/pages/rental-agreement). We assess fairly and communicate before any charges.

## Gear

**How do I choose the right backpack size?**
Each backpack listing includes capacity and recommended torso size. Contact us if you are unsure — we are happy to help.

**What if an item I want is unavailable?**
The date picker shows real-time availability. If your preferred item is booked, we may suggest a similar alternative — contact us for help.

**What is included with each rental?**
Each product page lists exactly what is included (and what is not). Typical inclusions: stuff sacks, repair patches, rain covers where applicable.

**Can I rent for a trip outside Tbilisi?**
Yes. You can take gear anywhere in Georgia. Delivery and return pickup are within Tbilisi only — if you leave Tbilisi for your trip, coordinate return pickup at your Tbilisi address before or after your trip.
