## Cleaning protocol

Before each rental, every item goes through these steps:

### Step 1: Return inspection
When gear comes back from a rental, we inspect it immediately for damage, missing parts, and cleanliness.

### Step 2: Deep clean
- **Tents and fabric:** Wiped down, aired out, spot-cleaned; rainfly and groundsheet checked
- **Backpacks:** Emptied, vacuumed, straps and buckles cleaned
- **Sleeping bags and pads:** Aired, spot-cleaned; pads wiped and checked for leaks
- **Cooking gear:** Washed, sanitized, dried completely
- **Hard goods (poles, stakes, tools):** Wiped, inspected, counted

### Step 3: Function check
- Zippers tested on all tents and bags
- Pole sections connected and tensioned
- Stove ignition tested (without fuel loaded for transport)
- Headlamps and electronics: batteries checked or replaced
- Water filters: flow rate tested

### Step 4: Dry and store
All items are fully dried before storage to prevent mold and odor.

### Step 5: Pre-delivery inspection
Before your delivery, the assigned items are inspected again and packed with all accessories verified.

## 15-point inspection checklist

Every item passes this checklist before going out:

1. All components present and accounted for
2. No tears, holes, or broken seams
3. Zippers functional
4. Buckles and clips intact
5. Poles/stakes complete set
6. Fabric clean and odor-free
7. Waterproof coatings intact (tents, rain gear)
8. Straps and harnesses undamaged (backpacks)
9. Insulation loft adequate (sleeping bags)
10. Pad holds air (inflatable pads)
11. Stove functions (ignition, valve)
12. Electronics working (headlamps, GPS)
13. Labels and safety tags present
14. Stuff sacks and accessories included
15. Overall condition grade assigned

## Condition grades

We grade every item honestly so you know what to expect:

| Grade | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Excellent** | Like new — no visible wear, full function |
| **Good** | Light cosmetic wear — fully functional, may have minor scuffs |

We do not rent items that are worn out, unsafe, or past manufacturer lifespan.

## What we do not rent

For your safety, we retire items that:
- Have structural damage (torn fabric, broken poles, failed zippers)
- Are past manufacturer recommended lifespan
- Have expired safety certifications (e.g., climbing hardware)
- Cannot be fully cleaned or sanitized
- Are missing essential components
