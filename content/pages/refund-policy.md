# Refund Policy

**Page title:** Refund Policy
**URL handle:** refund-policy

---

## Overview

[Your Brand] wants you to be satisfied with your rental. This policy explains cancellations, refunds, and how we handle payment issues.

---

## Payment method

All rentals are paid **cash on delivery**. No online payment is collected at checkout. This means:

- No charge is made when you place an order
- You pay in cash when gear is delivered
- Refunds apply to deposits or pre-payments only (if applicable)

---

## Cancellations

| When you cancel | Policy |
|-----------------|--------|
| 48+ hours before scheduled delivery | Free cancellation, no charge |
| Within 48 hours of delivery | 50% of rental total may be charged if gear was reserved and prepared |
| Same day / no-show at delivery | Full rental charge may apply |

To cancel, contact us at [email@yourbrand.ge] or [+995 XXX XXX XXX] as early as possible.

---

## Date changes

Date changes are free if requested 48+ hours before delivery and the item is available for new dates. Contact us to modify your booking.

---

## If gear is not as described

If delivered gear does not match its listed condition or is missing components:
- Tell us before accepting delivery — we will replace or adjust on the spot
- If discovered after delivery, contact us immediately — we will arrange exchange or partial refund

---

## Security deposits

If a security deposit was collected:
- Returned within **48 hours** of return pickup
- Full refund if gear is returned complete and undamaged
- Partial deduction for damage beyond normal wear (per [Rental Agreement](/pages/rental-agreement))
- You will receive an itemized explanation before any deduction

---

## Late returns

Late returns are charged at the daily rental rate for each additional day. No refund is issued for unused days if you return early, unless agreed in advance.
