## What to expect on delivery day

1. Our team arrives within your selected time window
2. We present your gear for inspection
3. Quick ID verification (Georgian ID card or passport)
4. You sign the rental agreement (emailed to you in advance)
5. You pay the rental total in **cash**
6. We hand over your gear

**Please have ready:**
- Valid government-issued ID
- Exact cash amount (shown in your order confirmation)
- Someone available to receive the delivery (or delivery instructions in checkout notes)

---

## What if I am not home?

If no one is available at the scheduled time:
- Contact us as soon as possible to reschedule
- We offer one free reschedule per order
- Repeated missed deliveries may incur a re-delivery fee of 5 GEL

Add delivery instructions at checkout (gate code, building entrance, leave with concierge, etc.).

---

## Return pickup

Return pickup is **free** and included with every rental.

**How it works:**
1. We contact you 1 day before your rental end date to schedule pickup
2. Our team arrives at your address at the agreed time
3. We inspect each item on-site
4. If everything is in order, your rental is complete
5. Any deposit is returned within 48 hours (if applicable)

**Please have ready:**
- All rented items, complete with accessories (poles, stakes, stuff sacks, etc.)
- Items in reasonably clean condition (shake out dirt, empty pockets)
